Documents Required

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You will need a valid passport or national ID card, and a valid driving license. If your license is not from an EU country, you may also need an International Driving Permit (IDP).

Is an International Driving Permit (IDP) required?

An IDP is required for drivers with licenses issued outside the European Union. EU driving licenses are accepted without an IDP.

How long must I have held my driving license?

You must have held your driving license for at least 2 years. Some premium vehicles may require 3 or more years of driving experience.

Age Requirements

What is the minimum age to rent a car?

The minimum age to rent a car is 21 years. Some premium and SUV categories may require a minimum age of 25 years.

Is there a maximum age to rent a car?

Yes, the maximum age for renting a car is 75 years. Drivers over 70 may be asked to provide additional documentation.

Is there a young driver surcharge?

Drivers aged 21-24 may be subject to a young driver surcharge of €5-10 per day depending on the vehicle category.

No, we do not require a credit card. All payments are made in CASH at the time of vehicle collection. This is our unique model that makes renting easier for everyone.

We accept payments in Albanian Lek (ALL), Euro (EUR), US Dollar (USD), and British Pound (GBP). We recommend paying in EUR or ALL for the best rates.

Vehicle Selection

What types of vehicles do you offer?

We offer a wide range of vehicles including Economy cars, Compact sedans, SUVs, Premium vehicles, and Minivans. All vehicles are regularly maintained and serviced.

Which car should I choose for Albanian roads?

For mountain areas and countryside exploration, we recommend SUVs or vehicles with higher ground clearance. For city driving and coastal roads, any vehicle category works well.

Can I request a specific car model?

You can request a specific model, but we guarantee the car category, not the exact model. We display "or similar" for each listing as availability varies.

Cross-Border & Travel

Can I take the car outside Albania?

Yes! Our vehicles are authorized for travel throughout Europe. However, you must inform us in advance and may need to purchase Green Card insurance (€25) for border crossings.

Is ferry travel permitted?

Yes, ferry travel to Greece (Saranda-Corfu) and Italy (Durres-Bari) is permitted with prior notification. Please let us know when booking.

What about mileage limits?

Most of our vehicles come with unlimited mileage. For vehicles with limits, the daily allowance is typically 300km with additional kilometers charged at €0.15/km.

Yes, one-way rentals are available. There may be an additional fee depending on the pickup and return locations.
