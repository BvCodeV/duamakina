Documents required

Passport · Driving license

Bring your original passport (not just a photo) and a driving license held for at least 2 years. EU, UK, US, CA, AU, NZ, JP, CH licenses are accepted directly. Other licenses need an International Driving Permit. We photograph documents at pickup; copies are stored for 12 months and then deleted.
