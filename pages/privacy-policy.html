Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Demo notice: this website is currently for demonstration and testing only. Booking requests submitted through this website are not real reservations and do not create a rental contract.

Who We Are

Dua Makina is an Albania-based car rental website concept. You can contact us at duamakina@outlook.com.

Current Website Status

The website is not currently operating as a live booking platform. Any forms, prices, vehicle availability, booking confirmations, or inquiry emails are for testing and demonstration purposes unless Dua Makina separately confirms otherwise in writing.

Personal Data We May Collect

  • Contact details such as your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Driver details such as driver name, age, and licence expiry date.
  • Rental inquiry details such as pickup and drop-off locations, dates, vehicle, extras, selected countries, and special requests.
  • Technical and anti-spam data such as hCaptcha verification tokens, browser information, analytics events, and local storage preferences.

How We Use Data

We use submitted data to test the inquiry flow, respond to messages, prevent spam or abuse, improve the website, and understand basic website usage. We do not sell personal data.

Third-Party Services

The site may use Supabase for database and storage, EmailJS for inquiry email delivery, hCaptcha for spam protection, Vercel Analytics for basic website analytics, and jsDelivr for content delivery. These services may process data according to their own privacy terms.

Cookies And Local Storage

The website may use local storage or similar browser storage for selected language, currency, booking search details, cached vehicle data, and inquiry cooldown checks. Third-party services may use cookies or equivalent technologies for security, analytics, or delivery.

Data Retention

Because the site is in testing mode, inquiry data should only be kept as long as needed for testing, support, abuse prevention, or legal requirements. Before the site goes live, retention periods should be reviewed and documented.

Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or withdrawal of consent for your personal data. To make a request, contact duamakina@outlook.com.
